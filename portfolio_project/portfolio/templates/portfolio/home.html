The Neckar River in Heidelberg, December 2015

Ten days of walking and visiting Christmas markets in Bavaria. We were hoping for snow, but the temperature remained consistently between 4 and 8 degrees the whole time. Rothenburg was particularly impressive, with lots of medieval building, and a town wall surrounding most of the old town. We also stayed in Wurzburg and Heidelberg for 4 days each. A very attractive part of the country, which would be good to visit in the summer, as well.
